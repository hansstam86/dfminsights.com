FAQ

Common questions

What happens after I pay the $49?
You receive a confirmation email immediately. Once we reach 10 founding members, we begin building the paid platform (target: 8 weeks). You get early access before public launch. If we don't reach 10 members within 90 days, you receive a full refund — no questions asked.
Is this AI-generated or real engineering knowledge?
Both. Our system uses Claude (Anthropic's AI) with specialist prompts for each engineering discipline — Mechanical, Electrical, RF/Antenna, Battery, Software, Manufacturing. For the $500 Expert Review tier, a human senior DFM engineer reviews and supplements the AI output before delivery.
Is my PRD and BOM kept confidential?
Yes, unconditionally. All uploaded documents are stored encrypted, isolated per user account, and never shared with or used to train any AI model. A formal NDA is available on request for Expert Review clients.
What is a "project"?
A project corresponds to one hardware product under development. It covers all three build stages (EVT, DVT, PVT), the planning session, and your 10 engineering questions. If you're building multiple products, you use one project slot per product.
What if I need more than 10 questions?
You can purchase additional question packs (5 questions for $9) or upgrade to Expert Review for a deeper dive. Unlimited question plans for teams will be available once the platform is live.
What types of hardware can you review?
Consumer electronics, medical devices (FDA 510(k), ISO 13485, IEC 60601), industrial IoT, EV components, smart home, wearables, robotics, and more. Our AI team covers RF/wireless, battery systems, embedded software, mechanical design, PCB layout, and manufacturing process.
Who runs this company?
DFM Insights is founded and chaired by Hans Stam, a hardware entrepreneur with deep experience in electronics manufacturing. Day-to-day operations — product, engineering, and finance — are managed by an AI management team running on Claude. This structure keeps overhead near-zero and lets us deliver expert-level DFM analysis at a fraction of traditional consultancy cost.
